Teaching March 10, 2026

Morning Devotion (6am-7am)

Dear brothers and sisters, we are pleased to inform you of the official launch of our morning devotion sessions starting this March 2026. Join us every morning from Monday to Saturday from 6am to 7am for a time of prayer, Bible reading, and meditation.

This is a unique opportunity to start your day in the presence of God, strengthen your spiritual life, and receive strength to face daily challenges. "In the morning, O Lord, you hear my voice; in the morning I lay my requests before you and wait in expectation." (Psalm 5:3).

Announcement March 5, 2026

April Baptism Registration

Registration for the April 2026 baptism is now open! If you have accepted Jesus Christ as your Lord and Savior and have not yet been baptized by immersion, now is the time to take this step of obedience.

The ceremony will take place at the Niger River (right bank, facing Yantala district). The registration deadline is April 15, 2026. Please contact the church office or one of the pastors for pre-registration and pre-baptismal teachings.
